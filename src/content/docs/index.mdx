## What is Wuhu?

Wuhu is an AI coding agent built for developers who want powerful AI assistance
without giving up control. It runs **locally on your Mac** as a native Swift
app — no Docker, no containers, no remote servers required.

<CardGrid>
	<Card title="Local-first" icon="laptop">

		full access to your local filesystem, tools, and environment.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Swift-native" icon="apple">

## Platforms

Wuhu is available on **macOS 15+** and **iOS 18+**.

- **macOS** — full-featured desktop app with terminal integration
- **iOS** — mobile companion for reviewing and steering agent sessions on the go
